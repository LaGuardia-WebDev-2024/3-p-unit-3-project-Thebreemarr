Recipe #1

Rice and Chicken

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
boneless, skinless chicken breast 1 1/4 pounds
vegetable oil 1 tablespoon
Cream of Chicken Soup 1 can (10.5 ounces)
water 1 1/2 cups
paprika 1/4 teaspoon
uncooked instant rice 2 cups
fresh or frozen broccoli 2 cups (6 ounces)

Step 1: Season the chicken with salt and pepper. Heat the oil in a frying pan over medium-high
heat. Add the chicken and cook for 6 minutes (until browned on both sides).
Step 2: Add the soup, water and paprika in the pan until boiling. Stir in the rice and broccoli, then
turn down the heat. Cover and cook for 5 minutes (until the chicken is done)
Step 3: Season to taste before serving.

Source: 15-Minute Chicken & Rice Dinner
